RENTERS

Be the City.
Live the World.

The Dominion Rocket is about freedom; a simple and affordable lifestyle that connects you to your community. The heritage restoration of the historic Dominion Hotel offers an urban living experience that provides all you need and everything you want.

Designed for ease of experience, one cheque provides for all your housing needs - furniture, wifi, cable and flat screen tv packages available for your individual requirements. In addition to efficiency kitchens in every unit, the Dominion Rocket provides our communal kitchen, dining room, and lounge on the the second floor, laundry facilities, bike parking, and the lobby and cabaret of the historic hotel available for everyday use and special event functions. Our Resident Card further connects you to the people and businesses in your neighbourhood while our on-site concierge service helps clear the way.

Be a part of history. Be a part of the Dominion Rocket.

HISTORY

Consider an era of exploration and wonder.

Stephen Jones had a grander vision and conceived of the Dominion Hotel, erected to serve the fashionable new industry of tourism built around rail and steamship travel. The Dominion Hotel grew as the Esquimalt & Nanaimo Railway and CPR Steamship Company opened Vancouver Islandâs resource economy to world markets.

Between 1890 and 1913, the hotel grew three times with prominent regional architect Samuel McClure drafting the 1900 addition and Thomas Hopper the 1913 addition. The hotel has been through many renovations since then as customer tastes and needs have changed. Today the hotel is reimagined as The Dominion Rocket, a vibrant living community that recalls a time when much of the world was unknown, and adventure was always a possibility...and still is.
