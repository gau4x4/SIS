Antti Lovag & Pierre Bernard

The Maison Bernard players

Antti Lovag is a habitologist for whom the house is a space of freedom where spheres and curves supplant right angles. His encounter with Pierre Bernard, adventurer and philanthropist, enabled him to successfully construct his first house.

Visit the house

photographic essay

Upon the occasion of its creation the Maison Bernard endowment fund asked photographer Yves Gellie to create a series of artistic color based around Antti Lovag’s architecture.

The endowment fund

Isabelle & Jean-Patrice Bernard

The Maison Bernard Endowment Fund’s purpose is the protection of Antti Lovag’s work with Pierre Bernard. Originally a family residence, the House is one of the most beautiful organic architecture achievements.

Odile Decq & Isabelle Bernard

THE RENOVATION PROTAGONISTS

The Maison Bernard renovation work supervised by architect Odile Decq in collaboration with Isabelle Bernard resulted in a contemporary interpretation of this 1970s-era architecture.

The artists residence

Presentation

Each year the Maison Bernard also hosts an artist-in-residence. The selected artist receives a scholarship and must create an original work fitting in with the house’s architecture and its environment. The resultant works will be exposed in situ.
